The Definitive Guide

By Ken Tannenbaum 2026 Edition 32 chapters

Everything you need to know about ChatGPT — from your first conversation to Agent Mode, Atlas, and the workflows behind serious daily use.

What you'll learn

Eight things you'll be able to do
by the end of this book.

01

Choose the right ChatGPT plan for your situation, and know when to upgrade or downgrade as your needs change.

02

Write prompts that get useful answers on the first try, instead of three rounds of clarification.

03

Use Custom GPTs and Projects to build reusable assistants for the work you do every week.

04

Run Agent Mode and Atlas confidently — knowing what they can do, what they can't, and where the failure modes live.

05

Integrate ChatGPT with your files, your calendar, and your existing tools without giving up control of your data.

06

Set up team and enterprise workspaces with sensible defaults, sharing rules, and audit visibility.

07

Recognize when ChatGPT is the wrong tool — and which model or platform you should reach for instead.

08

Build a personal practice that keeps improving as the tool itself does, instead of starting from scratch each release.
